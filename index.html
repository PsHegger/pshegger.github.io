About me

I am a computer engineering student at Budapest, Hungary since 2011, but I started programming back in 2005. My first experience was with Pascal, but I quickly moved on to PHP and C#. After that I learnt Java because I wanted to make mobile applications. Smartphones didn't existed at that time, but J2ME was a possibility to hack mobile phones.

When I first met with Android it was in a really early stage of the OS (Android 1.0 on a T-Mobile G1), and I had only a few minutes to play with it, but I instantly fell in love with it, so I diceded to learn to program for Android. When I got my first Android phone I quickly started to write more and more smaller applications, but I never made anything significant.

After a few years of making small utility apps for myself and my friends I got a job offer in Android developer position in a starting startup.

My work

I worked for 9 months to Calmarkr (it is now called tappointment) as a senior Android developer, but I had to leave it to continue my university studies.

Since that I'm working as a freelancer Android developer, and my works can be found on my Github repositories , in my portfolio, and on Google Play
