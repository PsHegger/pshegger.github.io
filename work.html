Tappointment

Tappointment is an online appointment booking system where you can find your hair dresser, dentist or almost any other profession which can use a booking system, and find the best time for both of you without endless calling of each other.

As the only Android developer in the team I was responsible for the whole Android application and I also helped with decision about the product, as well as helping out our backend developer sometimes.
